INGREDIENTS FOR FRIED RICE
Rice
Mixed Vegetables
Onion
Beef Liver
Thyme
Vegetable oil
Sweet corn
Green beans
Carrot

PROCEDURES

STEPS



FOR MORE DETAILS
  • Rinse the rice with cold water and pour it into a large pot.
  • Add some cup of water to a large pot and parboil
  • After 15mins, take down and wash
  • Put the rice back on fire with a little of the water of your seasoned and cooked chicken
  • Allow to cook for 5mins
  • Bring down
  • Put an empty pot on fire
  • Add your vegetable oil
  • Allow to heat
  • Add your sliced Vegetables(carrot,green beans,sweet corn etc.) and beef
  • Allow to heat
  • Finally, add your pre cooked fried rice and stir till satisfaction.
INGREDIENTS FOR OFE-OHA

Stock Fish

pepper and crayfish

PROCEDURES

STEPS

  • Wash your cocyam and boil till it is soft for pounding. Then you pound
  • Season your meat,stock fish and smoked fish to taste.
  • After 15mins, add your already pounded cocoyam to thicken.
  • Add your ogiri#
  • Allow to cook for 5mins
  • Add your pepper and crayfish
  • Add your oha leaf
  • Allow to boil then you serve with eba or pounded yam
